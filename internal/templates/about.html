Plain Text Nostr

About

A simple and fast Nostr web reader: keys and signing in browser, server crawls relay hints, caches events, and makes them available via SQLite.

You browse over HTTP and get HTML; the server does the graphing, filtering, websocketing, deduplicating, JSONing, and all the other stuff that drains your battery, toasts your device, and makes notes over nostr feel clunky and slow.
